OUR SCHOOL TRADITIONS

This page highlights the key traditions and annual events that shape the student experience and contribute to the rich culture of Maryhill Girls High School.

Alumni Day

Our school host a valuable Alumni Day, providing current students with a fantastic opportunity to connect and interact with former graduates.the day is filled with insightful presentation from the alumni, who generously share their diverse experience and journeys since leaving our class rooms. These interactions offer current students a glimpse into potential future pathways and the realities of life beyond school.

PrizeGiving Day

Annually, we commemorate our cherished prize-giving day, a special occasion dedicated to recognizing the outstanding performance of last year's graduating class. former students returns to school to be honoured with well-deserved awards based on there academic merit.The day is further enriched by engaging presentations from current students, showcasing the ongoing vibrancy of our school community. Beyond the formal celebration,the event fosters a warm and enjoyable atmosphere,allowing the returning alumni to reconnect with their former teachers and spend quality time interacting with the current student, bridging the past and present of our school.

Target Day

Target day at Maryhill Girls High School is centered around goal setting and achievement. Students actively engage in setting targets for individual subjects as well as an overarching academic goal. The day culminate in a series of presentations where students share their chosen targets, the strategies they plan to employ to reach them, and their initial reflections on the process. this interactive approach foster a sense of ownership over their learning and provide a platform for students to articulate their academic aspirations.
